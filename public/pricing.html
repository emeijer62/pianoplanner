Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Go for free?
Yes! Every new account starts with a 14-day free trial of all Go features. No credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to subscribe or continue with the Free plan.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, iDEAL, Bancontact, and SEPA bank transfer through our payment partner Stripe.
What happens to my data if I downgrade to Free?
Your data is always safe. If you have more than 25 customers, you'll keep all data but won't be able to add new customers until you're under the limit or upgrade again.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure European servers, and never share your data with third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time.
