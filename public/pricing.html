Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you only pay the difference. When downgrading, the new price applies from your next billing cycle.
What happens after the free trial?
After 14 days, you can choose to subscribe or continue with the Free plan. We never charge without your permission. Your data is always safe.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, iDEAL, Bancontact, and SEPA bank transfer through our payment partner Stripe.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure European servers, and never share your data with third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time.
What is Smart Suggestions?
Smart Suggestions uses AI to find the optimal appointment time based on your existing schedule, customer location, and travel time. It automatically suggests times that minimize driving and maximize efficiency.
What is Theater booking?
Theater booking allows venues (theaters, concert halls, schools) to book appointments outside your normal working hours – evenings, weekends, or custom hours you define specifically for these customers.
